CalCam

Features

Easy Picture Management

CalCam knows when you have an event and automatically saves it to the corresponding project so it is easy to find later.

Import Events

Simply add the events from your current calendar. Each recurring event gets automatically added to your events list, so no tedious adding is needed.

Many More

Archive your older projects, add a note to each picture taken, view your calendar...

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

CalCam does not collect any data from your device. You have full control over any content you create within the app and may choose to share it with others at your discretion. All data is stored locally on your device, so please remember to back up any important information.

Permissions

The app may request access to your calendar, camera, or gallery to enable certain features. These permissions are optional, and you can disable them at any time if desired.
